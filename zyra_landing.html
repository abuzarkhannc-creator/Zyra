FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

Will my customers know they're talking to AI? +
No. Zyra learns your exact tone, vocabulary, and style from your past conversations. It uses your emojis, your phrases, your warmth. Unless you tell them, customers will think it's you replying.
What if the AI sends a wrong reply? +
By default, Zyra shows you every reply before sending and waits for your approval. You can also switch on "auto-send" mode for specific query types you fully trust it to handle — like order tracking questions.
Do I need any technical knowledge to set it up? +
None at all. Setup takes under 10 minutes — you fill in a form, upload some chats, and scan a QR code to connect WhatsApp. If you can use Instagram, you can use Zyra.
Which languages does Zyra support? +
Hindi, English, Hinglish, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, and Gujarati. Zyra automatically detects the language your customer is writing in and replies in the same language.
Does it work with my existing WhatsApp number? +
Yes. We connect to your existing WhatsApp Business number via the official WhatsApp Business API. You keep the same number your customers already know.
Can I cancel any time? +
Absolutely. No lock-in contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your settings page at any time. Your data is yours and we'll export everything for you if you need it.
