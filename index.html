FAQ

Common Questions

How does Nuzy know what to reply? +

During onboarding, you teach Nuzy your brand voice, product details, and policies. It learns from your past conversations and adapts to your style. You can also review and approve replies before they're sent, so Nuzy gets smarter over time.

What happens if Nuzy doesn't know the answer? +

Nuzy automatically escalates complex questions to you. Things like refund requests, complaints, or unique inquiries are flagged for your review. You'll get a notification and can reply manually—Nuzy handles the routine stuff, you handle the important conversations.

Do I need technical skills to set this up? +

Not at all! Setup takes 2 minutes. Just connect your WhatsApp Business account or Instagram, tell Nuzy about your products, and you're done. No coding, no complicated configurations—it's built for small business owners, not developers.

Can customers tell it's AI? +

No! That's the whole point. Nuzy matches your exact tone, uses emojis the way you do, and replies like a human. We've tested with real D2C brands—customers can't tell the difference. It sounds like you, not a robot.

What if I want to reply manually sometimes? +

You're always in control. You can jump in and reply manually anytime, edit Nuzy's suggested replies before sending, or turn off auto-reply for specific conversations. Nuzy assists you—it doesn't take over.

How much does it cost compared to hiring a VA? +

A virtual assistant costs ₹15,000-25,000/month. Nuzy starts at just $4/month (₹330). Even our most popular plan at $15/month saves you over 95% compared to a VA—and Nuzy works 24/7, never takes breaks, and responds in under 2 seconds.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes! 14 days completely free, no credit card required. Test it with your real customer messages, see how it handles your specific questions, and decide if it's worth it. Most brands know within 48 hours that it's a game-changer.

What channels does Nuzy support? +

Currently: WhatsApp Business and Instagram DMs. These are where most D2C brands get 90% of their customer messages. We're adding support for Facebook Messenger and email soon.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel with one click from your dashboard. If you cancel within 7 days of your first payment, we'll refund you in full. Simple as that.
